Video Introduction

The video shows two histograms titled Employment for Disabled and Non-Disabled Americans. For 1991 it shows two bars being compared - one blue, and one grey. The blue one is labeled disabled and reaches 50.2%. The grey one is labeled non-disabled and reaches 94.4 %. For 2010 it shows the blue bar for disabled Americans at 41.1% and the grey bar for non-disabled Americans at 79.1%.
